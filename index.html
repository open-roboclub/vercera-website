FAQs_

How big can my team be?

The maximum team size allowed is 3 for Hackathon, Treasure Hunt and Ideathon.For remaining events the participation would be inidividual.

Can I participate individually?

Yes you can for events like Photography, Blog Writing and Vercera-CodeThon.Please visit the events section for more info.

When will it start?

Vercera 2023 will stretch from 18th February to 23rd February 2023.

Where will it be conducted?

Vercera will be conducted on the college campus of ZHCET, AMU - Aligarh.

Will there be food arrangement?

Oh-Yeah-Absolutely. You will be provided with proper meals on both days. Thanks to our sponsors.

How much does it cost?

It shall be notified

What are the criteria of shortlisting the teams?

The registered participants will be shortlisted based on their GitHub and LinkedIn profiles.

What do I bring with me to the event?

You are required to bring your school/college ID, laptops, phone, chargers, extensions.

What is the eligibility criteria?

You must be a student from any recognised University/College.
