Vercera Code-A-Thon

    It is a 2-hour programming contest which will test your logical, problem solving, and programming skills. This event requires individual participation. Join the contest here

×

BLOG WRITING

    It is an ingineous and creative blog writing competition based on any topic of your choice. Come up with your writing skills and get awarded. The submission link will be shared at the start of the competition and you can submit your blog before given deadline. This event requires individual participation.


×

IDEATHON

    A 48-hour team participation event in which participants will come up with an innovative and revolutionary idea and present it to the judges. Your team can have maximum size of 3. The idea themes will be given at the starting of the competition.


×

Computer Aided Design(CAD) competition

    It is a 48-hour CAD modelling competition where you come up with a CAD model that solves a given problem. The problem will be given at the start of the competition. This event requires individual participation.


×

Software Hackathon

    It is a 48-hour event where you will build a software based product to solve a given problem. The themes will be release at the starting of the competition. The maximum team size is 3 for the competition.


×

Lost City Quest Game

    An adventure game wherein you will hunt down the clues and find the lost city treasure using a given map. The event spans entire university area but begins from AMURoboclub. The maximum team size is 3 for the game.

FAQs

I have registered for the event. What next?

You are now a registered member of Vercera. Join the Whatsapp group and we will keep posting the details about each event there

How big can my team be?

The maximum team size allowed is 3 for Software Hackathon and Lost City Quest game, 3 for Ideathon. For remaining competitions the participation would be inidividual.

How would I get the instruction for the competitions?

Once you register, the further details regarding each event will be posted on our social media and Whatsapp group as the fest goes on. You can then directly participate in them.

Where will it be conducted?

Vercera will be conducted at Aligarh Muslim University at different venues as mentioned in the timeline.

Do I need to register for each event seperately?

No! It only costs Rupees 30 (for club members) and Rupees 150 (for non-club members) to be a registered member of the entire fest. After registration, you can participate in any event as you like.

Can I participate in ideathon, CAD and Hackathon parallely?

If you can manage these events parallely, yes you can participate in all of them.

How can we register as teams?

The team formation form will be shared one day before the respective event.

What do I bring with me to the event?

For every competition, you need to bring your college ID. Rest depends on the requirements of the particular event.

Where do I submit my blog for the blog competition?

The blog theme and the submission link would be shared on the competition day.

Where do I give the coding contest for Code-A-Thon

The contest link is available in the event description above

What is the eligibility criteria?

You must be a student from any recognised University/College.
